## The Glivenko-Cantelli theorem
The Glivenko-Cantelli theorem asserts that if we have a sample of *n* observation $$X_1,X_2,...,X_n$$ from an underlying distribution, the resulting distribution we get is an approximation of the underlying distribution where we got the samples.
