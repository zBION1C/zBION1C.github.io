## Central limit theorem

The central limit theorem states that if we have independent and identically distributed random variables, if we sample from them the distribution thath we get is the standard normal distribution, even if the variables themselves are *not* normally distributed.

### Example
The galton board experiment is a pretty experiment that shows this kind of behaviour.
